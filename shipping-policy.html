Shipping & Delivery Policy

Last updated: March 2025

1. Delivery Areas

We currently deliver across India. Delivery timelines may vary based on your location and pincode.

2. Delivery Timeline

  • Metro cities: 3-5 business days
  • Other areas: 5-7 business days
  • We will contact you before delivery to confirm your availability

3. Order Processing

Orders are processed within 1-2 business days. You will receive an Order ID and confirmation after placing your order. We will call you before delivery.

4. Cash on Delivery (COD)

For COD orders, please keep the exact amount ready. Our delivery partner will collect payment when the order is delivered.

5. Tracking

Once your order is shipped, we will share tracking details via SMS/email if available.
