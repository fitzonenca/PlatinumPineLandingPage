Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2025

1. Information We Collect

When you place an order, we collect:

  • Name and contact details (phone, email)
  • Delivery address
  • Payment information (processed securely via Razorpay)

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Process and deliver your orders
  • Contact you regarding your order
  • Improve our services

3. Data Security

We do not store your payment card details. All payments are processed securely through Razorpay. Your personal data is protected using industry-standard security measures.

4. Third-Party Services

We use our own order processing system (hosted on Netlify) to collect and store orders, and Razorpay for payments. Razorpay has its own privacy policy. We may use Meta Pixel for advertising analytics when you visit from our ads.

5. Contact

For privacy-related queries, contact us through the details provided on our website.
